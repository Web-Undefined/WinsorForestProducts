Species

Eastern White Pine

Eastern White Pine is our most commonly sawn product. It has a light brown (sometimes reddish) heartwood and nearly white sapwood that tends to darken with age. It is straight grained and easy to work. It is a very soft wood and may required extra care to avoid dents.

Common Uses: Construction Framing, Siding, Flooring, Fencing

Rot Resistance: Moderate decay resistance

Slabs

Wood slabs are able to be milled up to 40-inches wide. Typically slabs range in thickness from 1.5-inch - 4-inches but thicker or thinner slabs can be cut. Slabs can be cut with or without the live edge or a combination of both to meet your needs.
